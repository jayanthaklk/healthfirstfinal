All you need to know about Lung Cancer

  • Ubaid Khan (MBBS)
  • Oct 1, 2021

Lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer deaths in the US and across the globe even. Almost 159000 people died of lung cancer in the US in 2005 as per reports. Smoking damages the lungs in many ways. It disintegrates the alveolar sacs and leads to emphysema. Lung cancer is a lethal disease that progresses rapidly. It can be prevented by abstaining from tobacco and eating a healthy diet.
